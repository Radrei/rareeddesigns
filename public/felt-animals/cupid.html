Cupid

Pegasus

Materials: felt, buttons, yarn, thread

Cupid was inspired by Valentine's Day. My favorite thing about Valentine's Day is the red and white aesthetic, so that's what Cupid follows. Cupid is made with white glittery felt (though it is subtle and harder to see than the glitter on the red) and red yarn hair. I believe Cupid was my first pegasus/non-unicorn.
