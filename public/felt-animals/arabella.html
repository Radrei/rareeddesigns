Arabella

Horse

Materials: felt, buttons, yarn, thread, paper flowers

The concept for Arabella was inpsired when I was walking through JoAnn Fabrics and spotted the red, black, and white flowers that are in her tail and mane. I thought that they would go perfectly on a white horse with black hair.

To make the hair, I bought some ultra soft, 100% acrylic yarn, and brushed it out with a pet brush, then ironed it with my straightener. The tail is sewn on, but the mane is glued on. The flowers are also glued on.

The name Arabella just popped into my head after staring at her for a bit. She was my first smooth-haired horse, and also my first horse (ie, no wings or horn).
