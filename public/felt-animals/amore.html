Amore

Winged Unicorn

Materials: felt, buttons, yarn, thread

Amore was inspired by Valentine's Day, and meant to be color opposites of amore. My favorite thing about Valentine's Day is the red and white aesthetic, so that's what Amore follows. When coming up with Amore's name, I was trying to go with something love/Valentine's Day themed. Amore is Italian for love.
